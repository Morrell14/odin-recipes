QUESO PASTA

DESCRIPTION

As much as I love from-scratch meals, now is not the time! With limited time, I really appreciate those meals that come together quickly with as few ingredients as possible. Thankfully, I've got a tried and true stash of quick and easy dinner recipes I turn to, including this queso pasta that I'm sharing with you guys today!

INGREDIENTS

  • Pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Salsa con queso
  • Rotel tomatoes (tomatoes & green chiles)
  • Mexican Cheese Blend
  • Spices: kosher salt, black pepper, onion powder, cumin, chili powder, and garlic powder

STEPS

  1. Boil pasta according to the package directions in a large pot of salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot or deep skillet, over medium heat, add ground beef (season beef with salt, pepper, onion powder, cumin, and garlic powder).
  3. Cook beef until done and drain off the fat.
  4. Pour in Rotel tomatoes (undrained) and salsa con queso sauce.
  5. Stir in cooked pasta until coated.
  6. Sprinkle with parsley to garnish.
  7. Once the cheese has melted, plate and serve.
